Elastic Net

- definition:
  Combines L1 and L2 penalties.
  Loss = MSE + λ1 * Σ |w| + λ2 * Σ (w²)

- why to use:
  To balance feature selection (L1) and stability (L2).

- when to use:
  When dealing with many correlated features.

- advantage:
  Handles multicollinearity better than Lasso
  Performs feature selection while maintaining stability

- disadvantage:
  Requires tuning two parameters (λ1, λ2)
  More complex than Ridge or Lasso
