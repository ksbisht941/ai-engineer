RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error)

- definition:
  Square root of MSE.
  RMSE = sqrt(MSE)

- why to use:
  To get error in same unit as target while still penalizing large errors.

- when to use:
  When you want interpretability + sensitivity to large errors.

- advantage:
  Same unit as target variable
  Penalizes large errors

- disadvantage:
  Still sensitive to outliers
  Slightly harder to compute than MAE
