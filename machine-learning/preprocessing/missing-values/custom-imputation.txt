Constant / Custom Imputation – Quick Notes

Definition:
Missing values are replaced with a fixed or domain-specific value.

Why use it:
Allows control based on business logic or meaning of missing data.

Strategies:

* Replace with constant (0, -1, "Unknown")
* Use domain knowledge

When to use:

* When missing values have meaning
* Categorical data with unknown category
* Business-driven datasets

Example:
Missing City → "Unknown"
Missing Income → 0

Key points:

* Simple and interpretable
* No statistical assumptions
* Can introduce bias if misused
* Depends on domain knowledge
